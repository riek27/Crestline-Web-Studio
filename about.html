About AFRI TECH

Learn about our mission to bring quality electronics to Juba and our commitment to excellent service.

Bringing Dubai-Quality Electronics to Juba

AFRI TECH was founded in 2020 right here in Juba with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide access to high-quality, reliable electronics at competitive prices for the people of South Sudan.

We understand the unique challenges and needs of our community when it comes to technology. That's why we've established direct supply chains with trusted partners in Dubai, ensuring that every product we offer meets international quality standards.

Our team is passionate about technology and committed to helping our customers find the right solutions for their needs. Whether you're looking for a reliable mobile phone, need electrical installation services, or require technical support, AFRI TECH is here for you.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses in Juba and across South Sudan with accessible, high-quality technology solutions and reliable support services that enhance daily life and drive economic growth.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred provider of electronics and electrical services in South Sudan, recognized for our quality products, exceptional customer service, and community-focused approach.
